来

Simplified: 来

Traditional: 來

Pinyin: lai2

to come

hither (directional complement for motion toward the speaker, as in 回來|回来[hui2 lai5])

for the past (amount of time)

(between two verbs) in order to

(used after 得[de2] (or 不[bu4]) to indicate possibility (or impossibility), as in 談得來|谈得来[tan2 de5 lai2] (or 吃不來|吃不来[chi1 bu5 lai2]))
